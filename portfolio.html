About Me

Starting my professional career as a full-stack web developer. I'm skilled in many programming languages and frameworks related to both front-end and back-end development. I absolutely love how Ruby on Rails handles server-side operations and for that reason It's my main tool. For implementing web designs, I use my favourite CSS pre-processor SASS and GSAP animation platform. What about version control? GIT all the way. Beside that I have an advanced knowledge in jQuery, PHP, Bootstrap and fundamentals in React.js and Java.

I study at the Faculty of Informatics and Statistics, University of Economics in Prague where I get a good knowledge of business and marketing sector along with some developer practical skills such as building Oracle database system, configuring Apache server, programming in Java and data science fundamentals. Most of our school projects are team based which improved my communication and cooperation with colleagues.
